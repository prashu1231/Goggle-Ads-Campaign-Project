# 📈 DataEdge Academy - Google Ads Campaign Capstone Project

## 📌 Project Overview

DataEdge Academy is a Google Ads capstone project developed to demonstrate practical knowledge of digital marketing and campaign management. The project simulates a real-world advertising campaign for an imaginary EdTech platform offering professional certification courses in Data Analytics, Business Analytics, Power BI, SQL, and Digital Marketing.

The primary objective of this project is to design, implement, and analyze Google Ads campaigns to generate qualified leads while optimizing advertising performance through data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Campaign Objectives

* Generate qualified leads for DataEdge Academy courses.
* Increase brand awareness among students and working professionals.
* Achieve a Click-Through Rate (CTR) above 5%.
* Maintain Cost Per Lead (CPL) below ₹170.
* Demonstrate end-to-end Google Ads campaign management skills.

---

## 🚀 Campaign Types Used

### 1. Search Campaign

* Target users actively searching for educational courses.
* Focus on high-intent keywords.

### 2. Display Campaign

* Build brand awareness using banner advertisements.
* Reach potential learners across Google's Display Network.
